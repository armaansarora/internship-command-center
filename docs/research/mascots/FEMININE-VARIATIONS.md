# The Tower — Owl mascot, FEMININE round (GPT Image 2, one-shot hardened)

*The founder chose the **cream owl** (`selected/cream-owl-favorite.png`) as the front-runner and asked
to push it toward a **more feminine** read: friendly, welcoming, warm, inviting — gracious Art-Deco
grand-hotel hostess energy, never kawaii/Duolingo. These 4 keep her **exact** locked identity and vary
ONE lever each. Drafted by an 8-writer panel, then cut/fixed by a 3-judge adversarial pass (brand /
render-TD / systems). The 4 below are the survivors with every judge fix applied.*

**The locked owl (from `selected/cream-owl-favorite.png`):** slender adult owl · warm cream-and-ivory
plumage · gold Art-Deco chevrons down the chest · deep-navy ONLY on the swept-back lash-like brow-tufts,
wing-edges, and facial-disc frame · large warm **amber** eyes (deep honey-gold, dark navy pupils, gold
rim, catch-light) · small gold beak · thin gold perch bar · seamless flat navy backdrop. Palette: navy
`#1A1A2E`, gold `#C9A84C`, cream `#F5F1E8` + amber eyes.

**Two non-negotiable fixes the judges forced into every prompt:**
1. **Eyes stay the locked size — never enlarged.** Warmth comes from brow shape + soft lids + almost-smile,
   not bigger eyes. (Enlarging the eyes is the one knob that tips her into kawaii/baby/Duolingo.)
2. **Wings never read as arms/hands.** Any gesture stays a soft, low, feathered owl wing.

**How to generate (recommended):** open a fresh GPT Image 2 session, **attach
`selected/cream-owl-favorite.png` as a reference image**, then paste one prompt. The prompt is written to
work standalone too. Save renders as `fem-1-gaze.png … fem-4-crest.png` (variants `-a`, `-b`).

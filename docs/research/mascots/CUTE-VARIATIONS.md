# The Tower — Owl mascot, CUTE-FRIENDLY round (GPT Image 2, one-shot hardened)

*Correction round. The prior "elegant feminine" set (`FEMININE-VARIATIONS.md`) read **sultry/seductive** —
wrong. Target now: **cute + friendly** (bright wide-open eyes, open cheerful expression, endearing,
instantly likable), and each variation is a **genuinely different design language**, not the same owl
re-posed. Drafted by a 7-writer panel, cut/fixed by a 3-judge pass (anti-sultry cuteness lens · render-TD ·
brand distinctiveness). These 5 are the survivors, with every judge fix applied.*

**Kept identity (all 5):** clearly an owl · warm cream-and-ivory plumage · deep-navy accents (ear-tufts,
wing-edges, facial-disc frame) · subtle gold Art-Deco chevron · large round warm **amber** eyes (honey-gold,
navy pupils, gold rim, bright catch-light) · small gold beak. Palette ONLY: navy `#1A1A2E`, gold `#C9A84C`,
cream `#F5F1E8` + amber eyes. Isolated on flat navy. Must read at 16px.

**The anti-sultry locks in every prompt:** big WIDE-OPEN round eyes (never half-lidded), no eyelashes, no
arched/coy brows, an OPEN symmetric smile (no smirk), pupils centered looking straight ahead (no side-glance).

**Each one is a different MEDIUM:** flat vector · chibi 3D · clay/vinyl · geometric Deco emblem · painterly.

**How to generate (recommended):** fresh GPT Image 2 session, attach `selected/cream-owl-favorite.png` as a
reference (for palette/colour only — these intentionally change her *form*), then paste one prompt. Save as

## cute-1 · Cheerbeak  *(flat modern vector mascot — panel's unanimous winner, 9/9/9)*
```
Brand mascot for a premium app — clean logo-style packshot, 1:1.

Subject: a cute, friendly OWL as a clean MODERN APP MASCOT — a polished, scalable flagship brand character built from a FEW BOLD SIMPLE ROUNDED SHAPES: one big rounded-egg body, two short stubby rounded wings tucked close to the sides (soft mitten paddles — NOT arms, no elbows, no hands), a wide round facial disc, and two small rounded navy ear-tuft nubs on top. Unmistakably an owl: warm cream-and-ivory plumage as a smooth flat fill (feathers reduced to 2–3 simple rounded scallop hints on the chest), deep-navy accents (the two ear-tuft nubs, a clean navy edge along each wing, and one smooth navy band framing the facial disc), and a small simple gold beak (rounded triangle). The face is the hero and must read at 16px: TWO BIG, BRIGHT, WIDE-OPEN, perfectly circular oversized eyes — deep honey-amber irises, round dark-navy pupils centered and looking straight ahead, a thin gold rim, and one big bright white sparkle catch-light in each. A small symmetric upward "u" beak-smile, both corners even. Expression is cheerful, open, warm and friendly — perky and instantly lovable, the universally likable modern-brand sidekick. Faces forward, symmetric, sitting upright and happy. Lock the layout so it reads identically at 16px and 512px: the eyes sit on a horizontal centerline and occupy roughly 40% of the facial-disc width; the two ear-tufts evenly spaced. Keep the chest mark to EXACTLY two thin centered gold Art-Deco chevrons, large enough to stay one readable mark. NOTHING sultry or elegant: no half-lidded/sleepy eyes, no eyelashes, no arched or coy brows, no smirk, no side-glance, no slinky pose.

Render: flat modern vector-mascot / clean brand-illustration style — bold simplified geometric shapes, smooth solid color fills, crisp clean edges, gentle soft shading only (one soft contact shadow under the body, light interior shading for roundness), generous rounded corners. Polished and premium like a top-tier app logo character — never a cheap clip-art sticker, never photoreal, never 3D-glossy. Minimal detail by design so it stays sharp at a tiny 16px icon.

Colors: ONLY deep navy #1A1A2E, brushed gold #C9A84C, and warm cream #F5F1E8, plus warm-amber honey eyes (navy pupils). No other hues — no browns, no grays, no teal, no pink cheeks, no chrome, no metallic gradients, no neon. Gold is matte brushed gold, never shiny metal.

Light: soft, even, flat studio light; gentle roundness shading only; one soft contact shadow beneath the owl. No harsh highlights, no rim glow, no glossy speculars.

Frame: single owl, centered, generous breathing room, isolated on a seamless FLAT deep-navy #1A1A2E background — no scenery, no perch, no props, no ground line, no border. Clean and instantly legible shrunk to a 16px app icon.

No text, letters, numbers, logos, or watermark.
```

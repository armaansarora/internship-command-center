- Text: #F5F0E8 (warm ivory primary), #D4C5A9 (parchment secondary)

- Glass cards: bg rgba(255,255,255,0.03), backdrop-filter blur(20px)
- Fonts: Playfair Display headings, Inter body, JetBrains Mono data

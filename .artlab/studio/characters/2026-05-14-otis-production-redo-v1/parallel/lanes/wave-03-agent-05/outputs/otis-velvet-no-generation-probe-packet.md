## Strongest Wild Card

Keep a single "Velvet No" threshold-keeper probe for Otis: a warm, apologetic access-control pose where he politely blocks the elevator threshold while looking deeply prepared to help. This is the boldest usable opportunity because it gives Otis a product job beyond greeting: he can represent locked floors, missing permissions, incomplete setup, or "not yet" states without the UI becoming cold.

The risk is obvious: if pushed too far, Otis becomes a bouncer, security guard, grumpy gatekeeper, or Santa doing crowd control. The keep condition is that he reads as the same soft Santa-adjacent concierge, just with a boundary.

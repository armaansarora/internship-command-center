# Otis Ship-Ready Relationship QA Guard

Lane: wave-03-agent-03  
Use: apply before promoting any relationship-aware prompt into the parent production generation pass.

## Pass Condition

The sprite passes this lane only if Otis reads as the warm Lobby threshold keeper at mobile size, visually distinct from Mara Voss and Rafe Calder, with at most one quiet Rowan Vale access hint.

## Required Checks

- Identity: same approved soft Santa concierge identity, older warm face, full white beard, glasses, slight belly, natural asymmetry, lived-in human imperfections.
- First-read role: at 96 css px, viewer reads concierge/threshold before executive, sales, security, archivist, or mascot.
- New-user anchor: expression and posture lower pressure; hands receive, orient, or steady rather than command.
- Lobby compatibility: burgundy, cream, deep navy, aged brass, and warm skin/beard tones separate cleanly from brass, ivory, burgundy, and deep navy lobby lighting.
- CharacterStage compatibility: full body, transparent, centered, 20-30 percent safe padding, no cropped limbs, no cropped prop, face not occluded by hand or ledger.
- Material restraint: matte fabric blocks, simple aged brass, no glossy jewelry overload, no hyper-detailed fabric noise.
- Motion readiness: every gesture can freeze cleanly for reduced motion and can support a small idle/greeting/listening/talking loop.

## CEO Drift Failures

Fail the candidate if any of these appear:

- severe power suit or architectural shoulder line
- one dominant gold executive accent
- floor-to-ceiling skyline, boardroom, or podium language
- arms crossed in command
- chin-raised domination
- pointed briefing gesture
- expression that feels like a CEO masking vulnerability
- immaculate fake-perfect model polish

## CRO Drift Failures

Fail the candidate if any of these appear:

- forward athletic lunge
- red pen or stylus used like a weapon
- whiteboard or pipeline-board posture
- tactical jacket or loud sales-floor styling
- competitive grin
- gesture that feels like chasing conversion
- over-urgent body angle that makes the Lobby feel like a War Room

## Rowan Hint Guard

Allowed once, preferably in `working`:

- worn index-card bookmark tucked into the ledger
- old elevator key on the aged-brass keycard ring
- tiny pale card corner in pocket

Fail if the hint becomes:

- archive shelves
- library green wardrobe takeover
- half-turned archivist silhouette
- tool belt or tool-ring focus
- mystery symbols
- lore labels or readable text
- Rowan appearing in the image

## Pose QA

- idle: must feel like waiting with care, not guarding, judging, or commanding.
- greeting: must invite the elevator, not sell a funnel or welcome a VIP into a boardroom.
- listening: must lean toward the user's state, not assess the user from authority.
- thinking: must be practical and warm, not strategic theatre.
- talking: must explain in a compact gesture, not present.
- alert: must protect the threshold without aggression.
- working: must show ritual care, with one optional Rowan hint and no archive clutter.

## Outfit Prompts

Regular outfit: burgundy concierge jacket or cardigan with ivory shirt, dark tailored trousers, restrained brass name pin, simple keycard or small key ring. Matte fabric, no loud trim.

Formal outfit: deeper burgundy jacket, charcoal waistcoat, ivory shirt, one refined brass detail, polished dark shoes. More ceremonial, never theatrical.

Working outfit: burgundy knit layer with rolled sleeves or practical vest, ivory shirt, plain ledger or tablet, keys kept small. More active, still premium.

## Pose Prompts

Idle: balanced stance, weight gently shifted, hands visible near midline, expression attentive and calm.

Greeting: low open palm inviting the user through the threshold, other hand relaxed near ledger or side.

Listening: slight forward lean, one hand holding a small ledger close, eyes focused with patient warmth.

Thinking: hand near chin or glasses, elbow kept close to body, no finger clutter, soft puzzled kindness.

Talking: one restrained explaining gesture at chest height, mouth slightly open, still concierge-polished.

Alert: posture more upright, keys or keycard held low, expression protective but not alarmed.

Working: ledger or tablet held clearly with both hands or one hand supporting it, eyes warm and prepared.

## Ship-Ready Generation Notes

- Prefer individual full-body sprites, not multi-pose sheets.
- Target native 4096 px or better on the long edge before derivatives.
- Transparent RGBA is preferred. If unavailable, use a perfectly flat single-color background only as a temporary extraction path.
- Keep every sprite within one consistent character frame: same head size, belly proportion, glasses, beard mass, shoulder slope, and shoe height.
- At 96 CSS px tall, the silhouette should still read as older concierge with beard, slight belly, and one clear gesture.
- Every prop must be useful and small. Otis carries the moment; props should not become the design.
